Accessibility Statement for Riley Hendershot's Accessibility Site

This is an accessibility statement from Riley Hendershot, a WEB/UX student at Weber State University.

Measures to support accessibility

Riley Hendershot takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of Riley Hendershot Accessibility Site:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assign clear accessibility goals and responsibilities.
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance:

  • Level A
  • Level AA
  • Level AAA

Riley Hendershot Accessibility Site is partially conformant with WCAG 2.2 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Accessibility of Riley Hendershot Accessibility Site relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:
